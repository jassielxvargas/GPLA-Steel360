Operating Concept

We would receive the project contract drawings showing end reactions, along with a “rough” structural model— essentially LOD 300 with columns and beams only and no connections. The Dominican Republic team would perform the labor-intensive portion of the connection modeling and preliminary design work. Then, depending on workload, one or two senior engineers in the United States would review, finalize, and stamp the calculations as required.

Key Advantages

  • Connections are added directly into the model, reducing back-and-forth clarification and RFIs.
  • Turnaround time improves because modeling and engineering progress in parallel rather than sequentially.
  • Licensed engineers can focus on review, coordination, and decision-making instead of production tasks.
  • We gain meaningful cost efficiency while maintaining direct control over quality, standards, and priorities.
  • The team develops long-term familiarity with our preferences and becomes an extension of our organization.

Client Information Delivery Using Steel360

Steel360 allows us to deliver project information to our clients in a simple, organized way. Through a secure portal, clients can access:

  • Contract drawings
  • Shop and erection drawings
  • A 3D model they can move, rotate, and explore on a computer or iPad

This helps clients understand the work visually, reduces confusion, and supports better coordination conversations. Steel360 also centralizes documents, maintains version control, and keeps everything in one place instead of being spread across emails and shared drives.
